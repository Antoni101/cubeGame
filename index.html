Click on a cube and drag it to another cube to merge.

Merging cubes adds both levels together, forming a new upgraded cube.

Cube levels go from level 0 - 6. The higher the level the faster and more money it produces.

A level 0 cube can only be merged with another level 0 cube. It cannot be merged with cubes of other levels.
